00 IN SIMPLE TERMS
What is it?

A specialized crawler that scans job boards, identifies target vacancies in specific regions, and automatically finds the employer's direct contacts.

Who needs it?

Recruiters, B2B sales teams, and job seekers who want to skip the middleman and contact decision-makers directly.

How does it work?

Provide keywords and a city. The system scrapes vacancies, resolves company domains, and extracts verified emails and phone numbers from their websites.

What do you get?

A structured database of leads: Vacancy Title -> Company -> Website -> HR Email -> Phone Number.
